Jollof Rice

Jollof rice is a popular Nigerian dish made with rice, tomatoes, onions, and a blend of spices. Here's how to make it:

Recipe Information

Prep time: 15 minutes

Cook time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4-6 people

Difficulty: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 3 medium tomatoes (blended)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a pot and fry onions until golden.
  2. Add the blended tomatoes and cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Stir in curry powder, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  4. Add chicken stock and bring to a boil.
  5. Add rice, reduce heat, and turn until cooked.

Tips & Notes

Important tip: Wash the rice thoroughly before cooking to remove excess starch. You can also add bay leaves for extra flavor, and remember to taste and adjust seasoning as needed!
